Why EventGlow?

Learn how EventGlow will transform the way you interact with your calendar.

Never Lose Track of Time Again

With EventGlow, a simple glance down at the device lets you know exactly how long there is until the next meeting. The intuitively designed LED shines green when there are more than 30 minutes until your next event, yellow at less than 20 minutes, orange at less than 10 minutes, and red for the last 5 minutes. This minimal indicator allows you stay appraised yet remain focused on the task or meeting at hand.

Save Time and Effort with QuickLaunch

EventGlow's companion app seamlessly integrates with your preffered calendar, video conference, navigation apps and more! This allows you to launch the app corresponding to your next event with just a tap of the top button. Zoom meeting coming up? A press of the button will launch zoom and allow you to quickly join your meeting, the same will work with Teams! Lunch plans across town? A quick press of the button launches Google Maps (or Apple Maps) with navigation open to your lunch destination.

Read More

Keep on top of your To-Do List

Double tap the side button to launch task manager mode. EventGlow's companion app integrates with your task manager app of choice to show you your progress throughout the day. A double tap of the side button will show you the status of your to do list for the day on the LED, with red signifying no completed tasks, yellow signifying 2 tasks left, and finally green signifying completion. Customize the LED codes in the EventGlow app to show exactly the colors you want for a certain number of tasks complete.

Sleek and Portable Design

EventGlow's minimal and stylish design makes it a perfect addition to your workspace. The unobtrusive design means you can keep it plugged in to your laptop and move around easily, allowing you to track your calendar no matter where you're working from.
