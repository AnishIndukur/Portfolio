Colorful LED Indicator

EventGlow uses a color gradient on its LED to show you how much time you have until your next calendar event starts. With a quick glance, you can know if your next event is just around the corner or still a while away.

One-Button Launch

Just tap the EventGlow button to automatically launch the corresponding app for your next event. Zoom Meeting Next? A single button opens the zoom app. Meeting Across Town? Tap the button to automatically get directions and travel time.

Integrated Task Manager

Double tap the side button to bring up task manager mode, where the LED shows how much progress you've made through your tasks today. EventGlow automatically integrates to your task manager to keep up to date with your busy day.

Stylish and Minimal

EventGlow's sleek design and minimal design make it an unobtrusive and stylish addition to your setup. EventGlow lives in your laptop's USB port so you can plug it in and forget about it.
